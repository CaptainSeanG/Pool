What should a healthy pool look like?

Targets tuned for this pool and your climate.

FC

3–6 ppm
Live happily around 4 ppm.

pH

7.4–7.6
7.8 is watch-it territory, not panic territory.

TA

70–100
80–90 is a nice calm zone.

CH

250–400
PebbleTec usually likes more calcium than vinyl pools.

CYA

30–50
Too much stabilizer makes chlorine lazy.
